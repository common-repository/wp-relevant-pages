Remove the extra page that generates Wordpress!

== Description ==

You can safely choose the types of pages that are not needed on your site. Instead, they will generate error 404.

Plugin removes from the site such as all kinds of unnecessary pages the author's archive, archives days / months / years or pages investments. Also concerned and the appropriate RSS feeds.

== Installation ==

1. Unzip files.
2. Upload 'wp-relevant-pages' to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory.
3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
4. Go to your 'Reading'.
5. Go to your 'Hide pages'.
6. Note the types of pages that you want to hide.
